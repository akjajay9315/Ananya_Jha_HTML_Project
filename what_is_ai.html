What is AI?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) refers to the ability of a computer or a robot controlled by a computer to perform tasks that normally require human intelligence. These include learning, reasoning, problem-solving, understanding language, and perception.

AI can be broadly categorized into two types:

1. Narrow AI: This is AI designed for a specific task like facial recognition or a chess-playing bot. It's what we mostly use today.

2. General AI: A more advanced form that can perform any intellectual task that a human can. This type of AI still doesn't exist but is a goal for researchers.

Common technologies behind AI include machine learning, deep learning, natural language processing, and robotics.
